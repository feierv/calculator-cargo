# Rail Service – Summary

Brief summary of the rail groupage offer: tariff period, pricing rules, transit times, and Romania consolidation options.

---

## Tariff & Period

- **Mode:** RAIL (rail freight).
- **ETD window:** 01–28 February (and warehouse cutoffs before 2026 Chinese New Year).
- **Goal:** Increase cargo volume and strengthen the business relationship.

---

## Service Rules (Clear Explanations)

### 1. Minimum volume

- **Rule:** Minimum charge is **1 CBM** (cubic metre).
- **Meaning:** Shipments under 1 CBM are still billed as 1 CBM.

### 2. Heavy cargo (weight per CBM)

- **Under 300 kg/CBM:** Normal (volume-based) pricing.
- **Over 300 kg/CBM:** Treated as **heavy cargo** (different rate).
- **Over 500 kg/CBM:** Charged by **weight** using the rule **1 CBM = 500 kg** (so weight is converted to “chargeable volume” at 500 kg per CBM).

### 3. Overweight handling (single package)

- **3–4.5 tonnes per package:** Handling fee **USD 230 per package**.
- **4.5–6 tonnes per package:** Dimensions must be confirmed in advance; handling fee **USD 530 per package**.

---

## Schedule & Transit Times (T/T)

- **Frequency:** About **1–2 trains per week**.
- **Transit times:**
  - **Chengdu → MALA:** 10–15 days *(MALA = **Małaszewicze**, Poland – main China–Europe rail gateway on the EU border; first stop before Budapest/Bucharest.)*
  - **Chengdu → Budapest:** 18–21 days.
  - **Chengdu → Bucharest:** 25–32 days.
- **Cutoff:** To secure a given ETD, cargo must typically be **at the warehouse / picked up 10 days before** that ETD.

---

## Service Advantages

1. **Small built-in batteries:** Shipments with batteries **under 100 Wh** can be accepted by rail.
2. **Sanctioned cargo:** Experience in handling sanctioned cargo by rail; in 2025 no containers were returned for this reason.
